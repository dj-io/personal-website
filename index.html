About Me


I'm a Software Engineer based in New York, NY, passionate about shaping the digital landscape through innovative software. With a strong front-end focus and a solid foundation in modern backend technologies, I bring a full-stack perspective to building intuitive, impactful applications.
My journey into tech began with a long-standing interest in software and the hardware that powered it. In June 2020, I completed a software engineering course with Per Scholas, a non-profit technical bootcamp, where I transformed my curiosity into a career.
Soon after, I joined as an Associate Instructor for new cohorts, guiding students through debugging, pair programming, and project building—an experience that deepened my skills and fueled my commitment to helping others break into tech.
Today, I work as a Mid-level Software Engineer on an agile team, leveraging the latest technologies to build engaging, scalable solutions.
